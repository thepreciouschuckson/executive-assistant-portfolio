Strategic Proof of Work

Evidence of revenue growth, operational architecture, and high-stakes executive support.

The Revenue Multiplier: RFP/RFQ Success

The Problem: High-stakes international contracts were being lost due to minor technical non-compliance and missed submission windows.

The Methodology: I developed a "Bid Compliance Architecture" that treated every RFP as a project. This included a technical requirement audit, a stakeholder dependency map (who needs to sign what and when), and a 48-hour "fail-safe" submission buffer.

The Result: I maintained an 85% success rate across international and local photography/videography contracts, transforming the EA role from an administrative cost to a revenue-generating partner.

The Data Strategist: Market Intelligence

The Problem: Leadership was spending 10+ hours a week manually sourcing leads, leading to "founder burnout" and a stagnant sales pipeline.

The Methodology: I built a custom Lead Intelligence Pipeline in Notion. I moved beyond just names, categorizing 1,000+ prospects by Y-Combinator funding stage, strategic priority, and founder background to ensure the executive only engaged with high-conversion opportunities.

The Result: Reduced executive research time by 80% and provided a "ready-to-act" pipeline that fueled business development for two concurrent quarters.
